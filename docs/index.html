META ADS

Ad 1- [Lead Generation]
Campaign Strategy & Setup:
Lead Generation :  Testimonial Ad

This campaign was designed to generate high-intent consultation leads for a cosmetology clinic using patient testimonial content, while maintaining cost efficiency and strong ROI.


A Daily Ad Set Budget: of ₹250 was used initially, with gradual vertical scaling of ₹40 per day based on performance to maintain delivery stability.

Audience Targeting: was kept highly relevant and local, focusing on Thane and Navi Mumbai, as the clinic primarily receives patients from these locations.

Interest Targeting: Interest targeting around cosmetics, beauty, and skincare was used, along with Advantage Detailed Targeting, allowing Meta’s algorithm to further optimize delivery.
Total Spend:  ₹1,779
Leads Generated:  18
Cost per Result (CPR):  ₹98.85
Revenue Generated:  ₹20,000
Return on Ad Spend (ROAS):  ~11.2x

Creative 

To ensure budget efficiency, placements were restricted to Instagram Reels and Stories, aligning with the content format and avoiding unnecessary spend on other platforms.

A Rich Creative lead form was used to reinforce the testimonial visually and pre-qualify users, resulting in better lead quality.
A whatsapp link to the business account was also attached to the end of the form for direct messaging purpose.

The campaign successfully delivered high-intent leads at a low cost, converting into significant revenue for the clinic. Strong creative relevance, local targeting, and controlled scaling played a key role in achieving profitable results.

The ad creative was a patient testimonial Reel, showcasing a real acne transformation after treatment by Dr. Manish Ratnakar. This helped build trust, credibility, and emotional connection key drivers for medical lead generation.

Ad 2 - [Lead Generation]

Campaign Strategy & Setup:
Lead Generation :  By starting chats on whatsapp

This campaign was designed to drive high-intent WhatsApp inquiries for a limited-time HydraFacial offer (Buy 3, Get 1 Free), focusing on users most likely to convert quickly for a cosmetic procedure.


A Daily Ad Set Budget: ₹100 was used initially, followed by gradual vertical scaling of ₹40 per day, ensuring controlled spend while allowing the algorithm to stabilize and optimize performance.

Audience Targeting: Audience was hyper-local, targeting users from Thane within a 20 km radius of ensuring relevance and higher walk-in probability. The age group 21–45 years was selected to capture users actively investing in skincare and aesthetic treatments, with both genders included.

Interest Targeting: Targeting covered beauty, skincare, cosmetics, fashion, brides-to-be, engagements, upcoming birthdays, and fitness professionals, aligning the offer with users likely to seek event-based or maintenance treatments. Advantage Detailed Targeting was enabled to allow Meta’s algorithm to expand reach to similar high-intent users.

Total Spend:  ₹744
WhatsApp Leads Generated:  13
Cost per Result (CPR):   ₹57.24
Revenue Generated:   ₹7,000
Return on Ad Spend (ROAS):   ~9.4x

Creative









Placements were optimized across Instagram Feed, Stories, Reels, and Explore, maximizing visibility for a static offer-based creative. The ad used a high-conversion image format, clearly communicating the HydraFacial 3+1 offer, designed to attract attention quickly and prompt immediate action.


The campaign delivered cost-efficient WhatsApp leads with strong purchase intent, resulting in profitable conversions. Clear offer communication, localized targeting, and direct messaging played a key role in achieving strong ROI.

GOOGLE ADS

Kinzi Pharma
Client: Kinzi Pharma
Campaign Type: Google Search Ads


Client Overview : 
Kinzi Pharma is a pharmaceutical company engaged in supplying and distributing pharma products, including raw materials and capsules, catering primarily to wholesale buyers and distributors.



Campaign Objective & Strategy:
The primary goal of this campaign was to generate qualified traffic from users searching for wholesale pharmaceutical products and distributors. Since this was a B2B-focused campaign with intent-driven keywords, a Search Ads strategy was chosen to appear precisely when potential buyers were actively searching for relevant terms.

The bidding strategy was set to Maximize Clicks, allowing Google to efficiently allocate budget toward keywords with higher click potential during the testing phase.
Campaign Setup:

Campaign Type: Search
Networks: Google Search Network & Search Partners
Bidding Strategy: Maximize Clicks
Average Daily Budget: ₹200
Language: English

Location Targeting:
Primary Location: Dar es Salaam, Tanzania
This location was selected based on demand for pharmaceutical wholesalers and distributors in the region..

Keyword & Search Intent Targeting:

The campaign focused on commercial and transactional search queries, ensuring strong buyer intent. Some of the key search themes included:

Wholesale pharmacy suppliers
Pharmaceutical distributors
Empty capsule suppliers
Pharmacy wholesale near me
Sodium hyaluronate wholesale

These queries indicate users actively looking to source pharmaceutical products, aligning well with Kinzi Pharma’s offerings.


Campaign Performance Summary:

Impressions: 89
Clicks: 12
Click-Through Rate (CTR): ~13.5%
Average CPC: ₹106
Total Spend: ₹1,270

Despite a limited test budget and short duration, the campaign achieved a healthy CTR, indicating strong relevance between search queries and ad messaging.

Performance Insights:

The high CTR suggests effective keyword selection and ad relevance for the target audience.
The average CPC of ₹106 reflects competitive bidding in the pharmaceutical wholesale category.

Search terms triggered were highly aligned with B2B buying intent, validating the keyword strategy.
This campaign served as a testing phase, providing valuable data for future optimization, including keyword refinement, bid adjustments, and expansion into additional regions or products.
